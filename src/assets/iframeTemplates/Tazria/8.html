Naaman, général d’armée du roi de Syrie, était un homme considérable et en grande faveur chez son maître, parce que le Seigneur avait donné par lui la victoire à la Syrie; mais cet homme, ce vaillant guerrier, était lépreux.
Now Naaman, captain of the host of the king of Aram, was a great man with his master, and held in esteem, because by him the Lord had given victory unto Aram; he was also a mighty man of valour, but he was a leper.

{ב}  וַאֲרָם֙ יָצְא֣וּ גְדוּדִ֔ים וַיִּשְׁבּ֛וּ מֵאֶ֥רֶץ יִשְׂרָאֵ֖ל נַעֲרָ֣ה קְטַנָּ֑ה וַתְּהִ֕י לִפְנֵ֖י אֵ֥שֶׁת נַעֲמָֽן:

And he went in, and told his lord, saying: 'Thus and thus said the maid that is of the land of Israel.'
